## üìù Step-by-Step Submission Process

### Step 1: Create App in Play Console
1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console)
2. Click "Create app"
3. Fill in:
   - App name: **CashLink**
   - Default language: **English**
   - App or game: **App**
   - Free or paid: **Free**
   - Declarations: Check all applicable boxes

### Step 2: Set Up App Content
1. Go to **Policy** ‚Üí **App content**
2. Complete:
   - **Content rating** (IARC questionnaire)
   - **Target audience** (Adults)
   - **Data safety** (complete all sections)

### Step 3: Store Listing
1. Go to **Store presence** ‚Üí **Main store listing**
2. Fill in:
   - **App name**: CashLink
   - **Short description**: (use the one above)
   - **Full description**: (use the one above)
   - **App icon**: Use your 512x512 icon
   - **Feature graphic**: 1024x500px
   - **Screenshots**: Upload 2-8 screenshots
   - **Privacy Policy URL**: https://cashlink.business/privacy-policy

### Step 4: Pricing & Distribution
1. Go to **Pricing & distribution**
2. Set:
   - **Price**: Free
   - **Countries**: Select all or specific countries
   - **Content guidelines**: Accept
   - **US export laws**: Accept

### Step 5: Create TWA (Trusted Web Activity)
Follow the guide in `PLAY_STORE_DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md` to create the Android app using:
- Bubblewrap (recommended)
- Or PWA Builder

### Step 6: Upload App Bundle
1. Build your TWA using Bubblewrap
2. Generate signed app bundle (.aab)
3. Upload to Play Console ‚Üí **Release** ‚Üí **Production**
4. Fill in release notes

### Step 7: Review & Submit
1. Review all sections
2. Check for any warnings or errors
3. Submit for review
4. Wait for approval (usually 1-3 days)
